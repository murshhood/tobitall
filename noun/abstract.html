NOUN

Abstract Noun

An abstract noun is a word for something that cannot be seen but is there. It has no physical existence. Generally, it refers to ideas, qualities, and conditions.

Examples in words:

  1. Truth
  2. Lies
  3. Happiness
  4. Sorrow
  5. Time
  6. Friendship
  7. Humor
  8. Patriotism etc.

Examples in sentences:

  1. Truthfulness is a virtue that is rare nowadays.
  2. Honesty depends mostly on truthfulness and integrity
  3. Childhood is the best time to build it.
  4. Different people may have different ideas, opinions, and beliefs.
  5. But some virtues receive universal acknowledgment
