  1. Do you know that man? ‘Yes, I walk with …………………every morning?

  2. Have you seen the books? I have not seen …………….

    1. It

    3. They
    4. Him

  3. I’ve bought a new computer. Do you want to use ……………?

    1. Him

    3. That
    4. Those

    3. It
    4. Him

    3. Me
    4. Them

    1. That

    3. Them
    4. It

  8. My father has a new job, but …………… doesn’t like ………… at all.

    1. He, that
    2. She, it
    3. It, him

  9. There’s no need for you to come. I can carry the bags ……………

    1. Itself
    2. Herself
    3. Me
    4. Myself

  10. I have a friend ………. is a medical student.

    1. That
    2. He
