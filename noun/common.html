NOUN

Common Noun

A common noun is a name for something which is common for many things, person, or places. It encompasses a particular type of things, person, or places.

Examples in words:

  1. Country: (it can refer to any country, nothing in particular),
  2. City: (it can refer to any city like Ibadan, Mumbai, Lagos, etc. but nothing in particular).

So, a common noun is a word that indicates a person, place, thing, etc. In general and a proper noun is a specific one of those.

Examples in sentences:

  1. Alex is a wonderful player.
  2. Sydney Sixers is the team he plays for.
  3. He was not born in this country.
  4. Sydney is the city he lives in.
  5. He is a man of dream.
