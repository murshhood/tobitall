NOUN

Countable Noun

The nouns that can be counted are called countable nouns. Countable nouns can take an article: a, an, the.

Examples in words:

  1. Chair
  2. Table
  3. Bat
  4. Ball
  5. Water
  6. Money
  7. Sugar
You can say 1 chair, 2 chairs, 3 chairs.

Examples in sentences:

  1. I have a cricket bat in my closet.
  2. The bat is made of wood from a tree.
  3. My brother has a mobile phone
  4. The phone is made of plastic and metal.
  5. I need some water for the cake.
